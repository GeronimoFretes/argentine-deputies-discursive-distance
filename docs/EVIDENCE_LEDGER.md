# Evidence Ledger

Maps every claim that will appear in the report or presentation to its concrete
evidence. A claim with `status = available` or `approved` must have a real,
re-runnable `generation_command` and an existing `source_path`. A claim that is not yet
backed by a generated artifact stays `pending_pipeline`, `pending_validation`, or
`pending_analysis` — it is never marked `available` in advance of the artifact existing.

## Status values

```text
available           - the cited artifact exists and was generated by the cited command
pending_pipeline     - depends on a pipeline stage not yet implemented
pending_validation   - depends on QA/validation not yet performed
pending_analysis     - depends on an analysis not yet run
approved             - reviewed and accepted as final evidence for the claim
```
